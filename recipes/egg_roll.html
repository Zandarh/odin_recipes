Egg Roll Recipe

The Nigerian Egg Roll

The Nigerian Egg roll is entirely different from the famous Chinese Egg Roll. In Africa, we prepare our version of Egg Roll just as the name implies. It is a boiled egg wrapped in a mass of dough and deep-fried.

This Egg roll is often sold as street food in west Africa. However, Tea lovers can have this for breakfast. Also, you can serve it with a glass of cold juice for brunch; For me, I eat this any time of the day and I must add, it is a very filling snack.

Just like the Scotch Egg, the African Egg roll is deep-fried and also very easy to make. However, a key to achieving great success in making this recipe lies in the preparation of the dough; the dough will be a little wet and soft and a bit hard to work with, donâ€™t be too concerned about that at this stage.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 1 Tsp Baking powder
  • 1/4 Tsp Salt
  • 2 Tbsp Sugar
  • 1/2 Tsp Nutmeg
  • 2 Tbsp Margarine
  • 1 Egg beaten
  • 8-9 Tbsp Water
  • 5 Eggs Boiled

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, baking powder, salt, sugar, and nutmeg in a large bowl.
  2. Add the margarine and use your fingers to blend the margarine into the flour until crumbly.
  3. Stir in the beaten egg. The dough should look dry at this point.
  4. Begin to introduce the water a little at a time and work the dough till the dough is soft and sticky but still workable.
  5. Work the dough for about a minute, then cover it and let it rest for about 15 to 20 minutes.
  6. Work the dough again for a couple of seconds to a minute and divide it into five balls.
  7. Flatten out each ball of dough on the palm of your hand, drop the egg in the middle of the flattened dough and tightly wrap the dough around each egg.
  8. Fry on medium heat until golden brown about 6 to 8 minutes turning it every few minutes.
  9. Serve your Egg Roll hot or warm. Enjoy!
